Company Information

Review the Bank’s corporate overview, history, governing documents, and principal company information.

Company Overview

The purpose of the Bank shall be to engage in banking and investment activities in compliance with Islamic Sharia for its own account or on behalf of others, locally and internationally.

Company History

Banking and commercial activity of Al Rajhi Bank started more than 50 years ago. Individual establishments were merged into Al Rajhi Trading and Exchange Corporation and later converted into a Saudi joint-stock company.
